## Crystal Habits
- **Primary habit:** Pseudocubic to tabular crystals; also granular crusts, nodules, fibrous masses
- **Common forms/faces:** Pseudocubes from combination of rhombohedron and basal pinacoid; tabular on {0001}
- **Twin laws:** Not commonly twinned

- **Dehydrates?** No (already a hydroxide)
- **Radiation sensitivity:** None notable

## Paragenesis
- **Forms AFTER:** Pyrite, marcasite, pyrrhotite (the sulfides being oxidized). The death of sulfides is jarosite's birth.
- **Forms BEFORE:** Goethite, hematite, limonite (as pH rises, jarosite converts to iron oxides)
- **Commonly associated minerals:** Goethite, limonite, gypsum, anglesite, cerussite, malachite, azurite, chalcanthite, native sulfur, ferrihydrite, schwertmannite
- **Zone:** Supergene oxidation zone, acid mine drainage, gossan (iron cap). THIS IS THE GOSSAN MINERAL.
- **Geological environment:** Oxidized tops of sulfide ore bodies, acid mine drainage, acid sulfate soils, fumarolic deposits. On Mars: Meridiani Planum (Opportunity rover, 2004).
