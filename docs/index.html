🪨 Vugg Simulator

A text-based crystal growth engine

A vugg is a small cavity in rock where crystals grow in the dark over thousands of years. Temperature shifts, chemical pulses, and tectonic shocks leave their signatures in the crystal's growth zones — a geological record written in mineral.

This simulator lets you watch that process unfold, control it, or read the record afterward. Choose a mode:

🔬 Simulation

Play preset scenarios based on real geological settings. Watch crystals nucleate, grow, dissolve, and twin as temperature and chemistry evolve. The vugg tells its own story — you observe.

🏰 Creative

You control the variables. Adjust temperature, inject silica, flood with brine, trigger tectonic shocks. You have the freedom to decide the vugg's fate. Will you help it prosper, or will it sleep silently?

📀 Record Player

View a visual representation of a crystal's life journey. Like tree rings, the spiral pattern reveals repeating motifs that shaped the crystal into what it became.

🌀 The Groove

An idle vug that runs forever. Temperature drifts, chemistry shifts, crystals come and go. Leave it running overnight — come back to see what grew. Screensaver for geologists.

Welcome to the Vugg Simulator. Select a scenario and press Grow, or hit Random for a surprise. Switch to 🏰 Creative Mode for step-by-step interactive control.
