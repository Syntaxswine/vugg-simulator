🔬 Simulation

Play preset scenarios based on real geological settings. Watch crystals nucleate, grow, dissolve, and twin as temperature and chemistry evolve.

🏰 Creative

You control the variables. Adjust temperature, inject silica, flood with brine, trigger tectonic shocks. Decide the vugg's fate step by step.

📀 Record Player

Spiral visualization of a crystal's life journey. Like tree rings, the pattern reveals what shaped it.

🌀 The Groove

An idle vug that runs forever. Temperature drifts, chemistry shifts, crystals come and go. Screensaver for geologists.

🎲 Random Vugg

Roll a specimen. Random archetype, procedural chemistry, text-adventure preamble. A discovery because it is one.

Browse every mineral. Filter by class, fluorescence, or element. Reference — no simulation.

Welcome to the Vugg Simulator. Select a scenario and press Grow, or hit Random for a surprise. Switch to 🏰 Creative Mode for step-by-step interactive control. Scenarios: cooling — Simple cooling: hot fluid cools slowly in a vug pulse — Cooling with a mid-growth fluid pulse event mvt — Mississippi Valley-type: fluid mixing produces sphalerite + calcite + fluorite porphyry — Copper porphyry deposit: Cu pulses + oxidation → pyrite + chalcopyrite reactive_wall — Acid pulses dissolve limestone walls, feed crystal growth radioactive_pegmatite — Uraninite + galena in a hot pegmatite. Deep time. ☢️ radiation damage. supergene_oxidation — Cold, oxidized water-table zone. Pb+Mo+Zn+As → wulfenite + smithsonite + adamite + mimetite + goethite. ouro_preto — Imperial topaz veins, Minas Gerais. 360°C metamorphic brine, F-gated topaz nucleation. Cr³⁺ trace → golden-orange imperial color. gem_pegmatite — Gem pegmatite pocket (Minas Gerais). 650→300°C cascade: feldspar+quartz wall, schorl→elbaite transition, Be-gated beryl (enormous crystals), albitization event, late topaz.
