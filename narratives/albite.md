## blurb

NaAlSi₃O₈ — the sodium plagioclase end-member. At T < 450°C, albite orders to 'low-albite' (fully ordered Al/Si). Platy 'cleavelandite' habit is the pegmatite signature.

## variant: peristerite

Ca²⁺ intergrowth produced peristerite — fine albite/oligoclase exsolution lamellae that scatter light into blue-white adularescence. The moonstone shimmer.

## variant: cleavelandite

Cleavelandite habit — platy, lamellar blades curved like book-pages, the low-T hydrothermal signature.

## variant: twinned

Twinned on the {twin_law} — polysynthetic albite twinning creates the characteristic striped appearance of plagioclase in thin section.

## variant: dissolved

Acid released Na⁺, Al³⁺, and SiO₂ — albite is slightly more resistant than K-feldspar but still weathers to kaolinite under persistent acid attack.
