## blurb

PbWO₄ — tetragonal lead tungstate, the lead analog of scheelite (CaWO₄). Honey-yellow to orange-yellow Mohs 2.5-3 tetragonal crystals; the tetragonal polymorph dominant ~90% of the time over its monoclinic sibling raspite. Type locality: Cínovec (Czech Republic). Broken Hill (Australia) and Tsumeb (Namibia) produce the museum specimens — sharp honey-yellow dipyramids.

## variant: dipyramidal

Dipyramidal — {101} faces, the Broken Hill / Tsumeb display habit. Honey-yellow tetragonal symmetry visible at a glance.

## variant: tabular_default

Tabular — {001} plates, late-stage low-σ habit.
