## blurb

PbWO₄ — monoclinic lead tungstate, RARE. Same composition as stolzite but a different crystal system; stolzite (tetragonal) is favored ~90% of the time when both can form. Honey-yellow to brownish-yellow tabular crystals with perfect cleavage on {100}. Type locality: Broken Hill, NSW, Australia. The kinetic preference dispatcher in this engine reflects natural rarity — when you find a raspite specimen, it's the rare polymorph that the diffusion-limited geometry happened to favor in that particular vug.
