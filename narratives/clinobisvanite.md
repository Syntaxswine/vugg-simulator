## blurb

BiVO₄ — bright yellow to orange-yellow monoclinic Bi-vanadate. End of the bismuth oxidation sequence: bismuthinite → native bismuth → bismite/bismutite → clinobisvanite (if V is available). Microscopic — the crystals are individually sub-millimeter, so clinobisvanite appears as a powdery yellow coating on matrix.

## closing

And: BiVO₄ is a photocatalyst for solar-driven water splitting. The same mineral that forms as a supergene afterthought is being engineered to make hydrogen fuel from sunlight. Nature had it first.
